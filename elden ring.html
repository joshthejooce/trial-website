elden ring

story

the player takes the role of a tarnished someone exiled from the lands between in hopes of repairing the elden ring as the tarnsihed comes across melina who offers to take the place of the tarnisheds maiden to turn runes into strength and help the tarnished.

the tarnished is tasked with making its way across the land to find the elden ring but it was brocken into several fractions called great runes which very special demi gods were chosen in order to protect the great runes and become great rune holders.

once the tarnished makes its way through defeating the demi gods as it goes it comes across the erdtree the thing holding and keeping the elden ring but the entrance is blocked by thorns and the tarnished must make its way to the kiln of fire where malina sacrifices her life in order to set the erdtree ablaze and clear the passage for the tarnished.

once the tarnished has now slain all great rune holders it makes its way through the erdtree defeating all the protectors trying to stop this where insidethe erdree the incarnate of the elden ring is there and you must defeat it and after you do the true form comes out the elden beast.

once you defeat the elden beast you lie with the body of queen marika and what you do next is how it goes down but the main ones being letting it break to enter the world of darknes or put it together and become elden lord.

features

this is the first open world souls game.

you dont need to worry about conserving your healths as now you have a certain ammount of flasks and when you run out of them you can get them refiled by resting at a site of grace, as the game goes on you can aquire more flasks.

in this game you can find sites of grace around the world these are resting points where you can refil your flasks or level up but by resting at a site of grace all enemies come back to life except bosses and mini bosses

this game has a type of currency called runes these are gotten by killing enemies and used for buying things and leveling up but when you die you drop all your runes and if you dont get to them in time and die before you do then you lose your runes

this game has items that you can find that can give you a certain buff for a period of time such as maybe giving you a resistence to lightning etc
