dark souls

story

the world was once ruled by dragons known as the age of the ancients as a primordial fire known as the first flame and this flame creates four things called lord souls making anyone who weilds it incredible power and with this power the founders of these souls gwyn the lord of sunlight, nito the first of the dead, the witch of izalith and the furtive pygmy.

with this new power gwyn, nito and the witch kill the dragons taking over the world begining the age of fire gwyn sacrifices his life to protect the first flame.

because of the age of fire it can turn people who died and curse them bringing them back making them go through a proces called hollowing.

the player is one of the chosent that become cursed and has a goal to link with the first flame to keep the age of fire going, once the player gets to the end he is challenged to fight gwyn and when the player does he is shown with a choice to link with the fire and keep the age of fire going or let the fire die and enter the age of darkness.

features

unlike in demon souls you now dont need to worry about conserving your healths as now you have a certain ammount of flasks and when you run out of them you can get them refiled by resting at a bonfire, as the game goes on you can aquire more flasks.

dark souls has items that you can find that can give you a certain buff for a period of time such as maybe giving you a resistence to lightning etc.

in this game you can find bonfires around the world these are resting points where you can refil your flasks or level up but when you rest at a bonfire all enemies come back except for bosses and mini bosses

this game has a type of currency called souls these are gotten by killing enemies and used for buying things and leveling up but when you die you drop all your souls and if you dont get to them in time and die before you do then you lose your souls
